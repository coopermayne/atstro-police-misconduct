# Incomplete Test Case

## Basic Info
- Sarah Johnson
- March 10, 2024
- Oakland, Alameda County  
- Oakland Police Department

## What Happened

During a wellness check, Oakland PD officers forced entry into Sarah Johnson's apartment. She was experiencing a mental health crisis and had called for help. Officers tased her multiple times despite her not posing any immediate threat.

## Legal Info

Family filed lawsuit in May 2024. Still ongoing.

## Notes
This is an example of how mental health calls can go wrong. Officers had no crisis intervention training.
