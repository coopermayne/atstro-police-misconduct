## Key Facts
- June 13, 2024, around 7:45-8:45 PM
- Location: 14600 block of Cahita Avenue, West Rancho Dominguez (near Rosecrans Ave)
- Family members called 911
- Caller said Magana was "high on drugs" and "inhaling a substance from a canister"
- Magana was in backyard firing shotgun
- He made his way onto the roof of the home
- Fired 4-6 rounds at LASD helicopter
- Helicopter struck at least once - impact marks on helicopter blade
- Round narrowly missed aero crew
- Deputies tried to communicate, including calling his cell phone
- At one point he pointed gun to his head
- When he took another shot at helicopter, two deputies on ground opened fire
- Magana struck and pronounced dead at scene
- 12-gauge shotgun and ammunition box recovered
- Neighborhood on lockdown through 6 AM the next day

## Background
- 30 years old, resident of Compton
- Battled drug addiction
- Grew up playing soccer
- Had just been released from rehab at time of shooting
- Left behind a 6-year-old daughter
