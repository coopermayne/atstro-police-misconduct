- **Date**: March 19, 2024
- **Location**: Long Beach (near Bellflower border), Los Angeles County
- **Agency**: Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department (Lakewood Station)
- **Outcome**: Fatal - shot by deputies after charging with knife

## Key Facts
- March 19, 2024, around 9:18 PM
- Deputies responded to 15900 block of Indiana Avenue in Paramount for restraining order violation
- Woman reported Acosta came to her house demanding to be let in
- Deputies spotted Acosta driving orange 1970s Pontiac
- Followed him to intersection of E. Artesia Blvd and Downey Ave in Long Beach
- Three patrol vehicles behind him at red light
- Acosta put car in reverse, crashed into patrol unit
- Airbag deployed, deputy injured
- Acosta exited vehicle with knife (3-inch blade)
- Deputies ordered him to drop knife
- Acosta fell, got up, and charged at deputies
- Two deputies (Macias and Van Der Zanden) opened fire
- Acosta pronounced dead at scene
- One deputy hospitalized for crash injuries
