# Joseph Henson Case Notes

## Case Summary
- **Victim**: Joseph Westley Henson, 30, White male
- **Date**: February 23, 2024, approximately 6:45 AM
- **Location**: Shell gas station, 13000 block of Pearblossom Highway, Pearblossom (Antelope Valley)
- **Agency**: Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department (Palmdale Station)
- **Outcome**: Pending investigation (no DA report yet)

## Key Facts
- Henson entered convenience store and asked clerk to call 911 for ambulance
- Threatened to stab clerk if he didn't comply
- Deputies responded to "assault with a deadly weapon, person with a knife" call
- When deputies arrived, Henson was outside the store
- Deputies repeatedly ordered him to drop the object
- Deputy warned: "Please don't do this. Drop whatever is in your hand... If you come close to me I'm gonna shoot you."
- Henson continued advancing toward deputies
- Deputies shot him multiple times
- Transported to hospital, pronounced dead
- Sharp metal tool recovered (possibly screwdriver or pick)
