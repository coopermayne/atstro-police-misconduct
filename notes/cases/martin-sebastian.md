## Key Facts
- Martin Sebastian, 28 years old, Hispanic male
- Shot and killed by LASD deputies on March 16, 2024, around 9:20 PM
- Location: Near Gage and Eastman avenues, East Los Angeles (near El Tio Tono Market)
- Deputies responded to reports of a man harassing customers at the market
- Sebastian was armed with a metal stick and a replica firearm
- When confronted, he "produced what appeared to be a handgun"
- Deputies opened fire; Sebastian died at the scene
- Replica firearm recovered at the scene
- Around 10:45 PM, crowds formed attempting to engage deputies, forcing expanded perimeter
- California DOJ handling the investigation
