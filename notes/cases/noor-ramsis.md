# Noor Ramsis Case Notes

## Case Summary
- **Victim**: Noor Sherif Ramsis, 38, of Reseda
- **Date**: January 13, 2024
- **Location**: 1300 block E. Florence Avenue, South Los Angeles
- **Agency**: Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department (Century Station)
- **Deputies**: Jonathan Sandoval (shooter), James Becerra
- **Outcome**: DA found lawful self-defense (November 21, 2025)

## Key Facts
- Deputies were flagged down by witnesses reporting a robbery
- Ramsis was observed holding a firearm in his right hand
- Sandoval fired 6 rounds, 5 struck Ramsis
- Wounds: face, abdomen, lower back, left thigh
- Pronounced dead at Saint Francis Medical Center at 3:51 PM
- Toxicology: amphetamine, methamphetamine, phencyclidine (PCP)
- Had previously worked at Bell Gardens liquor store
