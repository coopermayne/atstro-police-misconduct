## Incident Overview

On March 15, 2023, LAPD officers responded to a disturbance call in downtown Los Angeles. The incident resulted in the fatal shooting of John Martinez, a 32-year-old Hispanic male.

## Timeline of Events

**9:45 PM** - Initial 911 call reporting a man with a knife threatening pedestrians on 5th Street.

**9:52 PM** - LAPD units arrived on scene. Officers attempted verbal commands to drop the weapon.

**9:58 PM** - After Martinez advanced toward officers while still armed, three officers discharged their firearms.

**10:05 PM** - Los Angeles Fire Department arrived and pronounced Martinez deceased at the scene.

## Investigation

The LAPD Force Investigation Division conducted a comprehensive investigation. Body camera footage from three officers was reviewed. The investigation concluded that officers acted within department policy.

## Legal Proceedings

No criminal charges were filed against the involved officers. The family of John Martinez filed a civil lawsuit against the City of Los Angeles in June 2023. The case is currently pending in Los Angeles Superior Court.

## Agencies Involved

- Los Angeles Police Department (Primary)
- Los Angeles Sheriff's Department (Assisted with scene security)
- Los Angeles County District Attorney's Office (Investigation review)

## Community Response

The incident sparked several community meetings in the downtown area. Local activists called for increased mental health response resources and de-escalation training.
