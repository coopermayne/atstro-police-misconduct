# {frontmatter.title}

## Incident Overview

Michael Chen, 28, died while in custody at the Orange County Jail on July 22, 2023. Chen had been arrested two days prior on suspicion of theft and was awaiting arraignment.

## Timeline of Events

**July 20, 2023** - Chen arrested and booked into Orange County Jail.

**July 22, 2023, 3:30 AM** - Cellmate reported Chen was unresponsive.

**3:35 AM** - Jail medical staff arrived and began emergency treatment.

**3:42 AM** - Orange County Fire Authority paramedics arrived.

**4:15 AM** - Chen pronounced dead at UCI Medical Center.

## Investigation Status

The Orange County Sheriff's Department is conducting an internal investigation. The Orange County District Attorney's office is conducting a parallel investigation. Preliminary autopsy results are pending toxicology reports.

## Medical History

According to family statements, Chen had a history of asthma and had requested his inhaler medication upon booking. Records indicate the medication was logged but family disputes whether it was properly administered.

## Custody Conditions

Chen was housed in a general population unit. He had no reported disciplinary incidents during his brief incarceration. Video surveillance from common areas is being reviewed, though individual cells are not monitored.

## Family Statement

Chen's family released a statement through their attorney expressing concern about the care he received while incarcerated and calling for a thorough investigation.

## Current Status

Investigation ongoing. No body camera footage exists as deputies do not wear cameras inside the jail facility. Surveillance footage and medical records are being analyzed.
